## Working takeaways

- The chapter argues that `antifragile` names the true opposite of `fragile`, while `robust` and `resilient` remain neutral rather than opposite.
- Damocles / Phoenix / Hydra provide the book's early map for fragile / robust / antifragile.
- Taleb treats the absence of a word as a perceptual handicap, using the history of color vocabulary and the example of blue in Homeric Greek.
- Mithridatization is presented as a robustness mechanism, while hormesis is a stronger case in which a limited dose of harm produces net benefit.
- Domain dependence is the habit of understanding an idea in one context and missing it in another.
