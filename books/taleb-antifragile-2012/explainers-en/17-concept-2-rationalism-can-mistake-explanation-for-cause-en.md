## Core idea

The demand for reasons often comes after practice and can misidentify what actually made the practice work. 

## Detailed explanation

This matters because Taleb is not just criticizing one philosopher. He is criticizing the whole tradition that treats verbal clarity as the test of real knowledge. 

Taleb is carrying forward the epiphenomena critique into philosophy itself. In the whole book, this sets up Book V, where Taleb formalizes why outcome structure matters more than narrative coherence.
