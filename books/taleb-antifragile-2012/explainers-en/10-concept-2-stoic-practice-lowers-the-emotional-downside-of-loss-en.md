## Core idea

By mentally degrading the importance of possessions and rehearsing loss, Stoicism reduces the damage inflicted by adverse outcomes.

## Detailed explanation

The method works before the bad event arrives. By practicing less dependence in advance, you shrink the emotional and practical downside of loss.

Taleb is turning Stoicism into applied risk management. This prepares the later via negativa chapters, where subtraction, restraint, and removal of dependence become practical tools.

## What this example is really showing

Taleb starts with Zeno, the founder of Stoicism. After a shipwreck destroys his cargo, Zeno treats the loss as release rather than total ruin. Taleb then gives Seneca-style modern scenes, like traveling with almost nothing or writing a resignation letter before starting a job so the job cannot own your mind.

The point of the scene: The method works before the bad event arrives. By practicing less dependence in advance, you shrink the emotional and practical downside of loss.
