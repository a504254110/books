Whole Book

The book in one minute

  • Taleb's central distinction is not fragile versus robust, but fragile versus antifragile.
  • The practical question is not only what will happen, but what kind of exposure you have if surprise arrives.
  • The book keeps returning to the same recipe: remove ruin, preserve upside, and stop overprotecting systems that need bounded stress.
  • The ethical end point is skin in the game: do not keep upside while transferring downside to others.
